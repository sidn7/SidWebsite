About Me

Hello! I’m a Lead Software Developer with over 6 years of experience building and scaling real-world software systems, and I hold a Master’s degree in Applied Computer Science from Dalhousie University, where I developed a strong foundation in software engineering and advanced computing concepts.

In my current role, I lead projects end-to-end—owning system design, driving key technical decisions, and ensuring high-quality delivery, while working closely with product and business stakeholders to translate requirements into practical, scalable solutions and mentoring developers across the team. My core expertise lies in backend development using Java and Spring Boot, where I design and build reliable, secure, and high-performance microservices, along with strong experience in Angular for creating responsive, user-friendly interfaces.

More recently, I’ve been working with AI-driven and agentic systems using LangChain, LangGraph, AutoGen, and Model Context Protocol (MCP) to build intelligent, autonomous workflows, with a focus on improving efficiency, automating processes, and solving real engineering problems.

I’m passionate about writing clean, maintainable code and building systems that scale, with a strong focus on engineering fundamentals, clear communication, and continuous improvement as both a developer and a leader. I’ve worked across the full software lifecycle—from design and development to deployment and optimization—and enjoy collaborating in agile teams, tackling complex challenges, and delivering solutions that make a real impact.
